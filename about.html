Jamie Waxberg: Creative Technologist

My college career began in Illinois State Univeristy's Graphic Design program, until my hunger for technical skill within the creative arts lead me to the Arts Technology program. Arts Tech allowed me to broaden my horizons while sticking to my design roots, giving me knowledge in a wide range of fields such as graphic design, web design and development, photography, video, social media, digital sound design, digital animation and 3D modelling, and basic programming for creative projects. With such a wide range of fields, the Arts Tech program taught me the importance of having the ability to learn new technical skills quickly and apply them to projects. This has showed me how to keep up with the trends of modern creative technologies, and how to always be at the forefront of the creative industry.
